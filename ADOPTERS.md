# Adopters

Organizations and individuals running Fgentic — evaluations, pilots, and production deployments. Being listed helps the project (foundation paths ask for named adopters) and helps you (visibility, priority feedback channel).

## Be listed

Publication is PR-based so the adopter controls the exact public wording:

1. Append one row to the table below. Use `evaluation`, `pilot`, or `production`, a `YYYY-MM` start date, and an optional public contact or case-study link. Remove the placeholder row when adding the first adopter.
1. In the PR body, confirm that the named organization or individual approved the listing and that every submitted fact is public. The project may ask the named contact to confirm before merging.
1. Optionally copy the [one-page case-study template](docs/adopters/template.md) to `docs/adopters/<name>.md`, then add it to [the case-study index](docs/adopters/index.md) and the documentation navigation. Keep confidential deployment details, personal data, credentials, and internal metrics out of the PR.
1. Optionally add an approved logo under `docs/assets/adopters/` and reference its source and display permission in the PR. A logo is never required. Trademarks remain the property of their owners.
1. Sign every commit for the [Developer Certificate of Origin](CONTRIBUTING.md#development-workflow), follow the normal PR template, and respond to factual-review requests.

If you prefer the maintainers to draft the change, open a public issue containing only the approved public facts. A PR and confirmation from the adopter are still required before publication.
